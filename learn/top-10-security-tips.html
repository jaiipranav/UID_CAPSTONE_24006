Top 10 Security Tips

Protecting yourself online doesn't have to be complicated. Following these ten essential tips can significantly boost your digital security.

  1. Use a Password Manager: Create and store long, unique, and complex passwords for every account.
  2. Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA): Add a critical layer of security beyond just your password.
  3. Keep Software Updated: Always install updates for your operating system and applications to patch security holes.
  4. Recognize and Avoid Phishing: Be skeptical of unsolicited emails, texts, and calls asking for personal information.
  5. Back Up Your Data: Regularly back up important files to an external drive or cloud service to protect against data loss and ransomware.
  6. Secure Your Wi-Fi Network: Change the default name and password of your home router and use strong WPA3/WPA2 encryption.
  7. Be Careful What You Share: Limit the amount of personal information you share on social media and other online platforms.
  8. Use a Reputable Antivirus Program: Install and maintain security software to protect against malware.
  9. Think Before You Click: Be wary of suspicious links, pop-ups, and online ads.
  10. Use a VPN on Public Wi-Fi: Encrypt your internet traffic to protect your data when using untrusted networks like those at cafes or airports.
